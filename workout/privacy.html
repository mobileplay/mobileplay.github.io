Privacy Policy for Quick Workout: BurnPilot

Last Updated:

BurnPilot Labs Pte. Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Quick Workout: BurnPilot application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect or store the following information:

  • Workout and progress data: workout sessions, streaks, completion history, calories or time estimates, favorite exercises, and achievement progress.
  • Profile and preference data: name, fitness goal, fitness level, height, weight, daily targets, reminder settings, sound settings, and app preferences.
  • Device information: device model, operating system version, app version, and technical diagnostics used to maintain app stability.
  • Purchase status: subscription or premium unlock state provided by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • Notification permission state: whether workout or wellness reminders are enabled on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide workout programs, exercise guidance, reminders, and progress tracking.
  • Personalize recommendations based on your goal, level, and settings.
  • Maintain premium access and restore eligible purchases.
  • Improve app quality, fix bugs, and understand general app usage.
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback.

3. Health and Fitness Information

The App may display fitness estimates and may request access to device health capabilities if you choose to enable related features. The App is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. You should consult a healthcare professional before starting a new fitness program if you have any medical concerns.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers that help us operate, maintain, or improve the App.
  • With Apple services for in-app purchases, subscriptions, and entitlement validation.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Some App data may be stored locally on your device. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

You can change app settings, notification permissions, and device permissions at any time through the App or your device settings. You can also delete local progress data from the App where supported.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the latest update date.
