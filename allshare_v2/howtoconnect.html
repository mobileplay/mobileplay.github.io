Samsung Screen Mirroring TV Guide

How to connect?

1) How do I connect to a TV device?

- You just need to connect your iDevice and TV device to the same wifi network, then open the app.

- Tap the connect button at the top right corner of the screen.

- Select you smart tv.

2) What types of devices are supported by the application?

The application supports the following types of devices::

  • Samsung TV
  • LG TV
  • Sony TV
  • Android TV
  • Roku & Fire TV
  • Smart TV

3) If your TV device does not show up when searching?

Please use the remote to turn the TV off and on again

4) If cast feature not working?

Disconnect and reconnect with TV Device
