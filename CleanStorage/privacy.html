Privacy Policy

for AI Cleaner

Last updated:

AI Cleaner Labs Co., Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") builds AI Cleaner to help users free storage safely with on-device analysis. This Privacy Policy explains what we access, what we use, and what we never collect.

1. What We Access

  • Photos Library (with your permission): We access photo and video assets so the app can detect duplicates, blurry images, large files, and cleanup candidates.
  • Purchase Status: We use StoreKit/App Store purchase status to unlock premium features.
  • Notification Permission: Only if you enable reminder notifications.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not upload your photos or videos to our servers.
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your media to train external AI systems.

3. How Data Is Used

Data access is used strictly to provide core app functionality, improve stability, and support subscription entitlement handling. Media processing is designed to run on device whenever possible.

4. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to protect user data and app integrity.

5. Children

AI Cleaner is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest effective date is shown at the top of this page.
