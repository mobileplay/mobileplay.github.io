Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2024

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect or store any personal information. All screen-casting occurs locally within your home network.

2. Crash Logs & Diagnostics

Anonymous crash logs may be sent to Roku's developer dashboard to help improve stability. These logs do not contain personally identifiable data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services.

4. Network Traffic

When you cast content, video data is streamed directly from your iOS device or an Internet source to your Roku. We do not intercept or store this data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and intentionally collects no data from them.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised Policy.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, please email privacy@yourdomain.com or mobilesecure.feedback@gmail.com.
