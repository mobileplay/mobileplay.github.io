Screen Mirroring Receiver

Turn your Roku into a wireless display for your iPhone or iPad. Cast videos, photos, games and live presentations in just one tap – no cables required.

Key Features

  • Instant screen and video casting over your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Smooth playback up to Full HD; supports adaptive bit-rate streams.
  • Simple, clean interface – launch and start casting immediately.
  • Works with any iOS app that supports screen broadcast.
  • 100% local: the app stores no personal data and displays no ads.

Get Started

1. Install this channel on your Roku.
2. Open the "Screen Mirroring Receiver" channel – you will see a "Ready to Cast" message.
3. On your iPhone/iPad, open the companion sender app and pick your Roku from the device list.
4. Start broadcasting – your screen appears on the TV instantly!
