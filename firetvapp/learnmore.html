Casting Receiver

Turn your Amazon Fire TV into a wireless receiver for your phone or tablet. Cast photos, videos, music and web streams, or mirror your screen in just one tap – no cables required.

Key Features

  • Receive photos, videos, and music on your Fire TV over your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Full-screen playback with support for HLS and adaptive bit-rate streams.
  • Screen mirroring via WebSocket and WebRTC.
  • Simple, clean interface – open the app and start receiving immediately.
  • 100% local: no sign-in, no ads, no in-app purchases, and no personal data collected.

Get Started

1. Install Casting Receiver from the Amazon Appstore on your Fire TV.
2. Open the app – you will see a "Ready to receive" message.
3. On your phone or tablet, open a compatible sender app and pick your Fire TV from the device list.
4. Start casting – your media or screen appears on the TV instantly!
