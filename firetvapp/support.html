Common Questions

  • Nothing shows up when I cast. Make sure your Fire TV and your phone or tablet are on the same Wi-Fi network, and that Casting Receiver is open on the TV.
  • "App not installed" or connection error. Reopen Casting Receiver on the Fire TV, then try casting again from your sender app.
  • Playback stops or stutters. Move closer to your router or reduce other heavy network usage; streaming happens over your local Wi-Fi.
