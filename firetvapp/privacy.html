Casting Receiver

Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Casting Receiver ("the App") does not require an account and does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. All casting and screen mirroring happen locally between devices on your own Wi-Fi network.

2. Diagnostics & Analytics

The App does not include analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. It does not gather usage statistics or transmit any diagnostic data to us or to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Network Traffic

When you cast, the App receives a media address (or a mirroring stream) from a compatible sender app on the same local network and plays it on your Amazon Fire TV. Media is streamed directly from the sender device or an Internet source you chose. The App does not intercept, record, or store this content.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and intentionally collects no data from them.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised Policy.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, please email mobilesecure.feedback@gmail.com.
