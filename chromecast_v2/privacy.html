Privacy Policy for Smart TV Cast

Last Updated: [March 5, 2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart TV Cast ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our casting application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • Device Information: TV model, OS version, IP address
  • Usage Data: Connection success rates, feature usage frequency
  • Network Information: SSID (Wi-Fi name), connection type

3. How We Use Information

Your data helps us:

  • Establish secure device connections
  • Improve casting performance and reliability
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Cloud service providers for essential operations
  • Analytics partners (anonymous usage data only)
  • When required by law enforcement

5. Security Measures

We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for screen mirroring
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure token authentication
  • Automatic session termination after 24h

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to collected data
  • Delete connection history
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable local network permissions

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Policy Changes

We will notify users of material changes via in-app alerts or email.
